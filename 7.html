April be testing my limits

Doing a short post about some of the things I did in April. Even though it was a busy month mostly focusing on end-of-the-semester assignments, projects, and general academics, I still had a bit of fun.

It started warming up quite a bit - it feels like summer. I won't go into detail, but I realized my fear of bugs was more intense than I realized. My cleats for rugby were falling apart so I got a new pair; they look pretty slick. I had two rugby tournaments but I don't have any good pictures from them so you'll just have to take my word that I played in them. They were taxing.

Those black buns were the highlight of the night. Salted egg custard buns - just magnificent

The only big thing that happened this month was a boat party a group of exchange students had to celebrate the end of the semester. I got extremely sunburnt, and felt it could have been a shorter day - but overall it was a fun day I won't forget. I jumped off the boat into the water, and I was not originally intending to do it because it was scary, but it ended up being really rewarding facing that fear. I swam in salt water for the first time, and it is indeed salty. I also wore my cool shirt and a cool pair of sunglasses, so I was having a good time. There was a mix of people I knew and didn't know, but overall it was a great group of people. I will miss the friends I've made throughout this exchange.
